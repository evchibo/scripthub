{$domain} is an Digital Marketplace. At {$domain}, you can purchase and sell digital files. Items are priced between $3 and $100; we review complexity, quality, and overall appeal when determining the specific value to assign to an item.

Finding and Buying Items

There are lots of ways to find the items you want:

  1. Category List – Browse {$domain} extensive category list here.
  2. Keyword Search– Access the search field on the top right of any page.
  3. Popular Files – Check out our weekly summary of the most popular items
  4. Collections – Browse our user-compiled item collections on a variety of themes
  5. Featured Files – Handx-picked by our site editors each week
  6. Top Authors – Look through the portfolios of our top authors

You can buy items by depositing cash. Once you have money in your account, just click the Purchase button in the top-right of any item’s description page. You’ll be taken from there to a file-download dialog. Download your purchased items at any time by going to your Account > Downloads page.

Item Support and FAQs

All items for sale on {$domain} are reviewed to ensure they are in good working order and are easy to use. Further item support such as alterations/enhancements is at the discretion of each author.
