Refer new users to our {$domain} Marketplace and you’ll receive 30% of their first purchase or cash deposit! Every user automatically has a referral code. Simply paste a link or image button on your site using that code. If a new user clicks your referrer link and proceeds to sign up an account and purchase an item or deposit money via any Envato Marketplace, you will receive 30% of that person’s first cash deposit or purchase price. So if they deposit $20 into their account, you get $6; if they deposit $100, you get $30; if they buy a $200 item, you’ll get $60!

How it Works

Here are a few important points about how it works:

  • We use cookies to track people who have clicked on your link, so they need to be using cookies for us to track them
  • If a person clears their cookies then we can’t track them any more
  • If a person clicks on your referral link and then later they click on someone else’s, yours is the one that counts
  • If a person clicks a link, they have three months before the cookie expires. If they sign-up and make a purchase, then you earn the commission. There is no time limit on the referral program.
  • Commissions appear in your cash account just like item sales
  • Linking from an Envato Marketplaces to itself in order to gain referrals is not allowed. This includes bouncing links of other domains in order to add a referral
  • If you have visited one of the Envato Marketplaces before and then click a referral link, it doesn’t count
  • Above all, use of the referral program is subject to a fair usage policy which gives Envato the right to review each and every referral

Your Referral Links

To use our program, simply add your username to the referral url. It doesn't get any easier!

Or, if you know what you're doing, simply add {$domain}/ref=username to the URL's query string, replacing username with your own username.

Note: Referral codes will not work on links to an item's fullscreen preview if the Marketplace preview bar at the top of the page has been closed.
